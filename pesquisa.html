Cosmopolitan

We are talking about a cocktail that has known success in the early years of this millennium. By drinking it from a distance we can only wonder: why did we stop drinking it? To prepare it, pour into the shaker, in this order: vodka, lemon juice, Cointreau and cranberry or currant juice. Garnish the glass with a slice of lemon. Drink in small sips.

Ruso Blanco

This cocktail is not for the indecisive. The palates that know how to drink White Russian are those that know how to choose it. Pour all the ingredients into a glass: ice, cream, coffee liqueur and vodka. Shake well and serve with a brandy cherry.
