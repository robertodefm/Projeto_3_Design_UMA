Moscow Mule

We could define it as the cocktail par excellence. The Moscow Mule, loaded with freshness, is back in trend from time to time and, although there are numerous variations, the original recipe is always the most popular. Here you can find the original recipe for the Moscow Mule.

  • 45 ml of vodka
  • 120ml ginger beer
  • 10 ml lemon juice
